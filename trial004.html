Recycled Plastics for Road Construction

At PETRACONNECT, we are pioneering the use of recycled plastics in road construction. By integrating plastic waste into asphalt, we enhance road durability, reduce environmental pollution, and create a sustainable solution for plastic waste management. This innovative approach not only provides stronger roads that withstand extreme weather conditions but also significantly decreases the carbon footprint of traditional road-building materials. Join us in paving the way for a greener future!

HISTORY

PetraConnect, founded on February 17, 2025, at the University of Energy and Natural Resources, is committed to reducing plastic waste through innovative recycling. The company transforms discarded plastics into valuable materials for road construction and manufacturing, offering a sustainable solution to environmental pollution. By pioneering the use of recycled plastics in road renovation, PetraConnect not only mitigates plastic waste but also enhances road durability. With a vision to revolutionize plastic recycling, the company integrates technology and sustainability to create a greener future. Through its efforts, PetraConnect is shaping a cleaner environment while promoting circular economy principles.
